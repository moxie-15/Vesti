    Frequently asked questions

    The AU Visa is available to nationals of most countries visiting for tourism, family, or short-term business. Requirements include: a valid passport (3+ months beyond travel), proof of funds, return ticket, and no intent to work. Citizens of UK, USA, Germany, and 50+ others may use an ETA instead.

    Online applications typically take 10–20 working days; paper applications take 25–35 days. Vesti recommends applying at least 6–8 weeks before travel. With Vesti Elitepass, you get priority document review to minimize delays.

    Standard requirements: valid passport, completed DHA form, passport photos, proof of funds (bank statements), travel itinerary, return flight ticket, travel insurance, and employment/study leave letter. Vesti's document packaging service helps organize and verify all of these.

    Elitepass ($150 USD/passenger) includes: 1-on-1 dedicated relocation advisor, pre-submission document review by an immigration specialist, private airport transfer on arrival in AU, and priority application routing within Vesti's system.
